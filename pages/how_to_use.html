How to use

TradeBoyAI allows you to trade smarter and faster. Access real-time cryptocurrency data and get insights on your next moves. Visualize updated charts and ask our AI about the market.

You can customise your charts to display the data you need in the way you need it. You can select a pair by using the "CRYPTOCURRENCY" and "FIAT" dropdown-menus.

Additionally, you can select a custom candle time length by using the "TIME-FRAME" dropdown-menu, as well as the time-range to visualize by selecting from the "CHART-LENGTH" dropdown-menu.

You can also create an account to keep track of your portfolio assets and save your favorite chart-views.
